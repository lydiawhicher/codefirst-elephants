ELEPHAS MAXIMUS

Status: Endangered
Height: Shoulder height is 10 feet
Weight: 5 tons
Habitats: Tropical and subtropical moist and dry broadleaf forests of India, Nepal, Bhutan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Myanmar, Thailand, Laos, Cambodia, Vietnam, China, Malaysia, and Indonesia


Asian elephant numbers have roughly halved in the last 60-75 years, and they are now restricted to just 15% of their historical range. Asian elephants are not quite as big as their African cousins, and have proportionally smaller ears.
